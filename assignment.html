Week 2 : Introduction -> 5 ring theory

5 ring theory introduction is a concept in which person introduces himself step wise. steps are as follows:

  1. Name
  2. Place
  3. Education
  4. Family
  5. Goal
Including these elements in your introduction creates a lasting impact on the listener and provides a brief yet meaningful insight about you to the interviewer.

Week 5 : Group discussion

Group Discussion (GD) is a structured form of discussion where a group of candidates is given a topic or situation and asked to share their views within a specific time. It helps assess participants' communication skills, teamwork, leadership qualities, critical thinking, and ability to handle pressure. GDs are commonly used in job interviews, college admissions, and competitive exams to evaluate how well individuals articulate ideas, listen to others, and contribute meaningfully to a team discussion. In this week’s lab session, we had an insightful group discussion with our classmates under the guidance of Sir Vishal Chabbra on the topic, "Amendments in the Judiciary for Women's Safety." Along with practicing GD skills, we also had the opportunity to reflect deeply on women’s safety in our country from a broader perspective. It was truly an engaging and meaningful topic for discussion.
